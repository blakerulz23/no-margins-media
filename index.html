Est. 2020 · Six active years

Crazy, creative and unique. We build communities around the art we make, artists first, always.

Founded in 2020 by Sensei Sand, shortly thereafter joined by Pikachu, and lastly by Ross Holmes in 2022.

Across six active years we've accumulated a plethora of music videos, visualisers, live music videography, photography and more, working closely with each artist to develop unique concepts made to showcase the intentions of every project.

Our number one priority is keeping the process smooth, efficient and, most importantly, fun. In any case, shoot day is always the best day.
